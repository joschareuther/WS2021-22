What does Weihnachten literally mean?
Weihnachten is originally a plurale tantum with the singular meaning Christmas.
This is still invariably used in wishes: Frohe, gesegnete, ...
... Otherwise, the word is most often treated as a neuter singular:
Weihnachten ist ein christliches Fest.
